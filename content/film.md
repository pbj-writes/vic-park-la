---
title: Filming in Victoria Park
---

Victoria Park is a semi-gated historic neighborhood in the heart of Mid-City Los Angeles. With its distinctive architecture, tree-lined streets, and residential character, the neighborhood has long been an attractive location for film, television, and commercial productions.

If you are interested in filming in Victoria Park, please contact our Filming Liaison Committee. The committee works to ensure that:

- Homeowners and residents near filming locations are properly notified
- Shoots begin and proceed smoothly
- Neighborhood disruption is kept to a minimum
- Communication between productions and residents remains clear and respectful

To help support the neighborhood and offset impacts, the Victoria Park Association encourages productions to provide a monetary donation to the Association. These contributions help fund neighborhood communications, safety initiatives, and community needs.

All filming permits are issued by the City of Los Angeles Film Office. The Association does not issue permits but serves as a liaison to help ensure a positive experience for both residents and production teams.
 
🎥 Homes in Victoria Park have served as locations for the following feature films and TV shows:

- L.A. Confidential (1997) - 4439 Victoria Park Drive 
- Insidious (2010) - 4350 Victoria Park Drive was used for the Lambert Home
- This Christmas (2007)
- Teen Wolf (2014 episodes) - 4336 Victoria Park Drive was used as Sean's house
- 9-1-1 (2018 episode) - 2143 S. Victoria Avenue 
- Modern Family (2012 episode) - The interior of 4311 Victoria Park Drive was used in the episode "Open House of Horrors". (The exterior was shot at the Miller and Herriott House in North University Park.)
