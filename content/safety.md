Safety is a top priority for Victoria Park. Our Safety & Security Committee works closely with:

- LAPD Senior Lead Officers
- City Council District staff
- Neighborhood block captains
- Local safety partners

This section will include:

- Ongoing safety updates
- Street lighting and infrastructure status
- Gate, access, and emergency coordination updates
- LAPD and community safety resources

For **non-urgent issues**: Contact [**311**](https://myla311.lacity.org) to file a report with the city.

For **suspicious activity or real-time emergencies**: Call **911** if you witness:

- Trespassing (e.g., someone climbing a fence into a neighbor's yard)
- Active crimes in progress
- Immediate safety risks

📌 Residents are encouraged to stay informed and report issues promptly using appropriate channels.

## How to Use 311 (City Services)

311 is the City of Los Angeles’ primary system for reporting non-emergency issues.

**Use 311 to report**:

- Street lighting outages
- Illegal dumping
- Graffiti
- Palm frond or bulky item pickup 
- Sidewalk or street hazards

### How to Submit a Request:

1. Visit [https://myla311.lacity.org](https://myla311.lacity.org); or
2. Use the MyLA311 app (iOS & Android); or
3. Call 3-1-1 from within Los Angeles

>💡 Tip
>Be as specific as possible when submitting a request and save your Service Request (SR) number for follow-up and share with our Communications team if items are not resolved in a timely fashion. Once submitted, email us at [victoriaparkassociationvb@gmail.com](mailto:victoriaparkassociationvb@gmail.com) to share your request #s & details for our neighborhood tracker. 

We periodically share best practices and neighborhood-specific guidance to help residents submit effective requests.
