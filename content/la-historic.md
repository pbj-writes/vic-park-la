---
title: Los Angeles Historic - Cultural Monuments
---

There are three Los Angeles Historic - Cultural Monuments in Victoria Park:

| Name & Address | Date Added | Description | Image |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 4318 Victoria Park Place | September 18, 1998 | Craftsman home built in 1912. Los Angeles Historic-Cultural Monument #654. | <img src="/media/Craftsman_Mansion.jpg" alt="Craftsman Home" width="200"> |
| Holmes-Shannon House, 4311 Victoria Park Drive | August 15, 2007 | Built in 1911. Described as "a residential building designed in the Tudor-Craftsman style by a prominent firm and reflective of the development of Victoria Park." Also listed on the National Register of Historic Places. Los Angeles Historic - Cultural Monument #885. | <img src="/media/Shannon_House.jpg" alt="Holmes-Shannon House" width="200"> |
| The Charles C. Hurd Residence, 4359 Victoria Park Place | August 12, 2014 | Built in 1909. Single-family home in the Arts and Crafts Tudor Revival style. Los Angeles Historic - Cultural Monument #1073. | <img src="/media/CharlesHurdResidence.jpg" alt="Hurd Residence" width="200"> |
