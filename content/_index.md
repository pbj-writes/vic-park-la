## Victoria Park Association

*A historic neighborhood committed to safety, connection, and stewardship.*

Victoria Park is a residential neighborhood in Mid-City Los Angeles, known for its historic homes, strong sense of community, and active civic engagement. The Victoria Park Association (VPA) is a volunteer-led group working to preserve the character of our neighborhood while addressing shared concerns around safety, infrastructure, and quality of life.

We collaborate with neighbors, city departments, and local partners to keep Victoria Park informed, connected, and thriving.
